Why Babylon is my favorite bedwars map

Babylon is very well-designed map in terms of build quality. Personally, there is nostalgic value to it, and I really like the color schemes and designs. My friend also prestiged on Babylon.

Strategically speaking:

• It is a large map, so you won't get immediately rushed after taking out the adjacent base.

• It is fast iron.

• Everyone who queues it is bad.

• There is a random, useless island, so you can predict if players are incompetent if they bridge there.

• It's big easy to clutch.
