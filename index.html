Why Babylon is my favorite bedwars map

Babylonis very well-designed map in terms of build quality. Personally, there is nostalgic value to it, and I really like the color schemes and designs.


Strategically speaking:

  • It is a large map, so you won't get immediately rushed after taking out the adjacent base.
  • It is fast iron.
  • Everyone who queues it is bad.
  • There is a random, useless island, so you can predict if players are competent or not if they bridge there.'
